About

Our PDF Helper app streamlines document handling by offering a summary function that condenses user's PDF into concise overviews, saving them valuable time. With this feature, users can quickly grasp essential information without sifting through lengthy documents. Additionally, the app enhances research capabilities by providing access to external resources based on keywords found within the PDF, providing deeper understanding and exploration of related topics, kickstarting working on projects and related.
